Frequently Asked Questions

How long until someone responds?
Response time varies by urgency. High/critical tickets are prioritized.
How do I track my ticket?
Use the Ticket ID on the Track page (Track Ticket link in navigation).
Can I attach screenshots?
Yes — screenshots will be supported in the admin panel soon. For now, include links or paste them in the message.
Who do I contact for urgent issues?
Use the phone number or email on Contact IT page during office hours. For system-wide outages, mark as 'Critical'.
